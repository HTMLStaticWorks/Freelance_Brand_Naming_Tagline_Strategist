Before You Ask

Common questions.

A few things clients tend to ask before starting a naming engagement.

Most brand naming engagements run between four and six weeks from signed brief to final delivery. This includes the discovery phase, linguistic exploration, concept testing, and presentation of the shortlisted names with full rationale.

For more complex projects — sub-brand systems, portfolio naming, or engagements requiring international cultural checks — the timeline is scoped individually. Rush timelines are available in select cases; reach out to discuss.

Typically three to four highly vetted name candidates are presented at the final stage — not a long list of fifty half-baked ideas. Every name that makes the final presentation has survived phonetic testing, preliminary trademark screening, and semantic association mapping.

The goal is quality over volume. You should be able to confidently move forward with any name on the shortlist.
