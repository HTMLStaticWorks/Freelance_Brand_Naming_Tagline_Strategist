The Methodology

How I Work

01

Discovery

Understanding the brand's core DNA, competitive landscape, and strategic objectives.

02

Exploration

Linguistic mapping, root word analysis, phonetic exploration, and semantic framing.

03

Testing

Filtering concepts through trademark screens, linguistic viability, and subjective appeal.

04

Delivery

Presenting the final shortlist with extensive rationale, context mockups, and usage guidelines.

My approach is rooted in linguistics and psychology. I don't believe in "brainstorming" names. I believe in engineering them.

Every syllable is intentional. Every letter combination is tested for rhythm, mouthfeel, and cognitive friction.
